In the enigmatic realm of Qarth, a planet divided between the Light and Dark sides, a shadowy specter casts a looming presence over the land. The dark side of Qarth is a place shrouded in mystery and dread, where the relentless rule of Empress Morana, known as the Tyrant of Twilight, threatens to engulf the entire planet in an eternal night of despair.

The Dark Side

The dark side of Qarth is a harrowing and enigmatic land, perpetually bathed in the eerie twilight that characterizes Empress Morana's dominion. The landscape is a labyrinth of twisted forests, ominous swamps, and desolate, light-starved wastelands. It is a place where even the very land itself seems to conspire against those who traverse it.

The Tyrant of Twilight, Empress Morana

Empress Morana is a figure of dread and fascination for those on the Light side of Qarth. Her origins remain shrouded in mystery, and her motivations are inscrutable. The history books call her an ageless being. There is never much detail, but the brief descriptions are all the same. Evil incarnate, encased in metallic black skin, with only the right side of her face looking remotely human. In her initial assault, Qarth was so unprepared that half of the world was conquered before forces could assemble to halt her progress.

The malevolent creativity of Queen Morana knows no bounds, as she has devised a particularly macabre and insidious weapon in her relentless quest to conquer the Land of Light. Her puppet-like soldiers, constructed from the remains of fallen Land of Light soldiers, are a terrifying testament to her dark sorcery and unyielding determination to expand her dominion.

The Macabre Soldiers

These grotesque constructs are an eerie fusion of technology and necromantic magic, a nightmarish blend of the living and the inanimate. They are not true undead, as they lack the consciousness and free will that characterizes most undead creatures. Instead, they are soulless automatons, mere tools for Morana to manipulate as she sees fit.

Construction

Queen Morana's puppet-like soldiers are painstakingly assembled from the remains of fallen warriors of the Land of Light. Limbs, torsos, and heads are reassembled into an eerie semblance of humanoid form. Dark, mystical bindings and rudimentary mechanical contraptions are then woven into their bodies, granting them a semblance of mobility.

Control

These nightmarish creations are under the direct control of Queen Morana herself. Through her malevolent sorcery, she can manipulate them with the precision of a puppeteer, making them march, fight, and obey her every command. It is as if the fallen soldiers are forced to serve their former enemies in an unending nightmare.

Terror and Psychological Warfare

Morana's puppet-like soldiers are terrifying not only for their grotesque appearance but also for the psychological impact they have on the defenders of the Land of Light. The sight of once-familiar faces and forms turned into abominations sows fear, confusion, and demoralization among the ranks of the Light side. This psychological warfare is just as potent as the physical threat they pose.

The Lands of Light

On the brighter side of Qarth, the Lands of Light bask in the radiance of the sun and are home to a diverse array of races and cultures. The denizens of this realm have long lived in harmony, safeguarding the fragile balance that exists between light and darkness. They are a people who value freedom, creativity, and the pursuit of knowledge. While they may not know much about the dark side, they understand that their way of life depends on protecting their borders from the encroaching forces of the dark.

The Lady of Light, Queen Saraphon

Queen Saraphon, the current ruler of the Forces of Light in the Land of Light, has inherited a perilous mantle of leadership, one that few before her have managed to hold onto for long. The position of power in the Land of Light's ruling circle is an alluring but deadly one, as the very nature of their leadership makes them prime targets for Empress Morana's relentless forces of darkness.

The Short-Lived Rulers

Throughout the history of the Land of Light, rulers have come and gone, their reigns often brief and fraught with danger. The darkness that looms on the horizon and the looming threat of Morana's dominion have led to a cycle of rapid turnovers in leadership. The forces of darkness relentlessly seek to eliminate these rulers, knowing that their demise can plunge the Light side into chaos and uncertainty.

Many have stood against The Empress, and many have failed, few of them noteworthy enough to be remembered after they are replaced. The first King however is still exalted in taverns across Solara and Helion, as the one reason Qarth ever stood a chance against The Empress. King Mathis, first of his name, a lowborn farmer who rallied the people of his small village to fight back against their assailants. He was the first to ever fell one of Morana's soldiers. He was living proof that this fight could be won.

News spread of his deeds, and the sheer willpower of Mathis drove the people of The Lands of Light to take up arms, forming the border between The Light Side and The Dark Side, between good and evil.

Solara

Solara somehow maintains its classy nature through the horrors of war. The citizens of Solara are almost exclusively high born, or of some political importance. If you do encounter a commoner they are likely a traveler from a neighboring village, or a shop keep of some sort. Solara is the most defended settlement in the Lands of Light, and houses Queen Saraphon herself.

Helion

Helion makes no effort to hide its true colors, the majority of the citizens you encounter are either older or very young, as most able bodied men and women are driven to the border to fight the ever raging war. Despite their unique makeup, Helion holds some of the richest traditions and happiest people. They make time for the small celebrations, the tavern songs and campfire tales.

Dawn's Rest

Dawn's Rest is a small village outside of Helion. Dawn's Rest hugs the outer wall of the main city, and is bordered on its other side by The Forests of Glendale. The village does not produce much other than it's share of crops, but makes up for it in it's ample housing. Dawn's Rest frequently takes on soldiers looking for places to rest on their way to or from battles.

The Borderlands

The borderlands between the Light and Dark sides are a perilous and contested zone. Those who dare venture into this treacherous expanse often return with stories that chill the hearts of the bravest souls. These tales speak of grotesque monstrosities, eerie apparitions, and the relentless, advancing darkness that threatens to devour all in its path.

For the forces of Light, a precarious peace exists, upheld by a vigilant order of defenders who stand as the bulwark against the encroaching darkness. They know that the time may come when they must go on the offensive to thwart Empress Morana's plans, but for now, they hold the line and prepare for the day when they must confront the Tyrant of Twilight and the horrors of the dark side of Qarth.
